About the Project

This project focuses on creating a speech-to-sign language translation platform, aiming to significantly improve communication for the deaf and hard of hearing community. It utilizes advanced technology to convert spoken language into accurate sign language gestures, displayed through animated avatars.

Key Features

Translator

  • Sign to Speech Conversion: Converts sign language gestures into Text or spoken words.
  • Speech to Sign Conversion: Translates spoken language into sign language animations.

Learning Platform

  • The Learning Platform is an educational hub dedicated to teaching sign language. It offers extensive resources, including video tutorials and practice sessions. Users of all ages and abilities can learn sign language at their own pace, enhancing communication skills and fostering inclusivity in the community.

    • Community Forum

    The community forum to engage with users. This platform fosters collaboration and the sharing of insights, making it a hub for exchanging ideas, solving problems, and driving innovation. Connect, learn, and contribute to discussions that shape the future of our field.

    Live Cam

    Live cams allow people to view happenings in distant locations instantly, offering convenience and real-time information.
